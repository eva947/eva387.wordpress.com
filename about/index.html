FAQs

What is your process for smaller projects?

We understand not every brand starts with a huge order. For smaller projects, we offer low-MOQ options and flexible customization (such as colors, packaging, or branding) so you can launch quickly without heavy upfront costs.

Who will I be working with?

You’ll work directly with our in-house R&D, design, and production team. We guide you step by step — from product concept and prototyping to certification and full-scale manufacturing.

How can I connect with others who have launched devices?

Many of our clients are founders and beauty brands like you. While we protect their confidentiality, we’re happy to share case studies, market insights, and best practices from successful launches so you feel supported throughout the journey.

Can my brand apply to develop a device with you?

Absolutely. Whether you need a private-label solution or a fully custom device, you can apply to start a project with us. Share your vision, and we’ll help tailor the right path to launch.
